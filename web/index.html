Nightell for Listeners

Nightell is your ultimate destination for captivating stories, and the best part? It’s completely free—always has been, always will be. Getting started is simple: sign up with your email or Google account, and you’re ready to immerse yourself in a world of storytelling.

As a listener, you have the power to curate your experience. Follow your friends to stay connected with their latest tales, or explore the voices of famous storytellers who bring stories to life in ways that will inspire, entertain, and move you. Whether you’re interested in personal experiences, thrilling adventures, or thought-provoking narratives, Nightell has something for everyone.

Our user-friendly search feature makes it easy to find exactly what you're looking for. Whether it’s reconnecting with a friend, discovering new stories, or diving into a specific genre or topic, Nightell helps you uncover the content that resonates with you. Simply type in a name, keyword, or topic, and let the stories find you.

Nightell isn’t just about listening; it’s about connecting with the stories that matter to you. Whether you’re listening on your commute, relaxing at home, or looking for inspiration, Nightell brings the voices of the world to your ears. Discover, connect, and enjoy—Nightell is here to make your listening experience truly extraordinary.


Nightell for Storytellers

At Nightell, we believe that every storyteller deserves a platform to share their voice—and we’re making sure it’s accessible to everyone, completely free of charge. Whether you’re a seasoned storyteller or just getting started, signing up is as easy as it is for listeners. Once you’re in, you can immediately begin uploading your voice recordings and sharing your stories with the world.

We're excited to announce that we're currently developing an advanced in-app voice recorder. This upcoming feature will offer powerful editing tools and enhancement capabilities, allowing you to refine your recordings directly within the app, ensuring your stories sound exactly how you envision them.

When you’re ready to share, simply upload your voice recording as a post, and your friends and followers will be able to listen and enjoy your story instantly. We support MP3 uploads up to 5MB, and to make your post stand out, you can add an optional photo that captures the essence of your story.

Nightell is designed to make storytelling as seamless and enjoyable as possible. Whether you're sharing a personal experience, a fictional narrative, or something entirely unique, Nightell provides the tools and audience to bring your stories to life. Start telling your story today and connect with listeners who are eager to hear what you have to say.
